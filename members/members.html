What You Get

Member Benefits

1

Priority Enrollment

Members get first access to new programs and class slots each term before public registration opens.

2

Cultural Events

Free or discounted access to Diwali, Holi, and all cultural celebration events throughout the year.

3

Progress Reports

Detailed termly progress reports and direct teacher feedback so you always know how your child is doing.

4

Community Network

Connect with other Wellington families who are passionate about Indian heritage, language, and culture.

5

Library Access

Borrow Hindi books, DVDs, storybooks, and cultural learning resources from our growing library collection.

6

Certification

Students who complete programs receive an official Paathshala Navoday certificate of achievement.

7

Performance Opportunities

Members can participate in our annual stage showcase, cultural displays, and community performances.

8

Parent Orientations

Dedicated parent sessions each term to keep you informed and involved in your child's learning journey.

Getting Started

How to Join

Joining Paathshala Navoday is simple and welcoming. Just four easy steps.

1

Contact Us

Call, email or fill in our online form to express your interest.

2

Choose a Program

We'll help you pick the right program and level for your child's age and experience.

3

Attend Orientation

Come along to a free parent orientation session to meet the teachers and see the school.

4

Start Learning!

Your child joins their first class and becomes part of the Paathshala community.
