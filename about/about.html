Our Vision

Old Teachings, New Methods

Paathshala Navoday was founded with a simple but powerful belief — that every child of Indian heritage in Wellington deserves a joyful, accessible way to connect with their roots.

We combine traditional Indian teaching values with modern, engaging classroom methods to make Hindi, Art, Drama and Cultural learning exciting for children and adults of all ages.

Our school is built on community, warmth, and the deep belief that language is the bridge between generations.

Parent Partnership

Parent Responsibility

As a parent, you are an integral part of your child's education. Each class is led by a certified teacher, but children thrive with parental support at home.

We believe the school-home partnership is the key to your child's success in language and cultural learning.

1

Attend orientation sessions

Join our termly parent orientation to understand the curriculum and expectations.

2

Support daily practice

Encourage 15–20 minutes of Hindi practice and cultural conversations at home.

3

Ensure regular attendance

Consistent attendance is key — please notify teachers of planned absences.

4

Help with assignments

Assist with worksheets, projects and creative activities between classes.

5

Stay in touch

Communicate openly with our teachers — we're here to help every step of the way.
